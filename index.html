Contributions for the Homeless


As of January 2018, more than 550,000 people were counted as homeless in the United States.

No one should be left alone.

Homelessness can happen to anybody.

We see it every day. In our commutes, a homeless pandhandles at a intersection. "Anything helps", as is the message. However the question remains: Will giving money to this man or woman feed an addiction? Am I helping or making it worse?

This is a common sight in the local community, and Tampa at large. Common enough that people have learned to adapt to their presence.
